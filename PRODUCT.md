# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Students, learners, professionals, teams, and educators using an AI-powered autonomous learning platform.

## Product Purpose
Project Marko is an intelligent, multi-agent learning platform that acts as a modern Bauhaus operating system for education. It aims to simplify complex workflows and AI system activity while maintaining a premium SaaS experience.

## Brand Personality
Modern, geometric, minimal, highly functional, and engineered. The interface should feel like a fusion of Bauhaus Design School, IBM Carbon Design, Braun (Dieter Rams), Linear, and Swiss Editorial Design.

## Anti-references
Avoid Glassmorphism, Neumorphism, heavy gradients, excessive shadows, decorative illustrations, rounded blob layouts, and visual noise. Avoid generic templates that don't employ strict editorial alignment.

## Design Principles
- **Form follows function:** Geometry over decoration, strict visual hierarchy.
- **Engineered Aesthetic:** Modular layouts, functional typography, editorial spacing, mathematical grid.
- **Purposeful Colors:** Neutral colors for 90% of the interface. Bauhaus colors (Red, Blue, Yellow) strictly for emphasis.
- **Every component communicates:** No decorative fluff.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard WCAG AA compliance. 4.5:1 text contrast minimum. High usability with visible keyboard focus and clear semantic HTML.
